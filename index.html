Deep Fried Oreos

Deep-fried Oreos are made with Oreo cookies, batter (usually made from buttermilk pancake mix and water), vegetable oil, and powdered sugar. Before preparation, the cookies are frozen for 3 hours. To begin, the oil is preheated to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
